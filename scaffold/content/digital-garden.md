---
title: Digital garden
---
A *digital garden* is a personal wiki of interlinked notes that grow over time.
Unlike a blog's reverse-chronological stream, a garden is a web you tend: notes
connect to one another, and the connections matter as much as the notes.

This starter garden demonstrates two of mug's features for that style of
writing: [[Wikilinks]] for connecting notes, and [[Backlinks]] for surfacing the
connections in reverse.
